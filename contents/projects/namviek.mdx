## Namviek: The Open-Source Task Manager Built for Developers

Namviek is a simple and open-source task manager designed specifically for the needs of developers. It offers a variety of features to help you stay organized and productive, including:

- Multiple views: See your tasks in different ways, such as a board, list, calendar, or dashboard, to find what works best for you.
- Google authentication: Sign in securely with your Google account for easy access and data synchronization.
- Custom statistics: Track your progress and identify areas for improvement with built-in statistics.

Whether you're a solo developer or working on a team, Namviek can help you streamline your workflow and get things done.
